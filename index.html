Key Features

Feature 1

Feature 1 enables users to make donations effortlessly, whether they are registered or unregistered. The donation form allows users to provide their basic details and donation amounts and select from a list of 8 causes they wish to support.

Feature 2

Feature 2 offers a volunteer feature where users can contribute in kind. Users can volunteer to provide resources such as vehicles for transporting donated goods or offer their services, such as cooking meals for children in need.

Feature 3

Feature 3 provides users with the ability to onboard the system and make recurring donations by subscribing to a temporal plan. Users can choose from various options, such as weekly, monthly, or daily donations, to continuously support the causes they care about.

About

My project was inspired by the sufferings of mankind (especially in Africa). This is a Portfolio Project to display my skills as a software engineer. I started this project because I want to alleviate suffering and mitigate the effects of poverty in Africa.
